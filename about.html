About Me

_____________________

Hi, I'm Paul, a passionate computer engineer with a strong background in FPGAs and microcontrollers, and a deep interest in embedded systems, mobile hardware design, and quantum computing. With over 3 years of hands-on experience, I specialize in designing and building innovative solutions that bridge the gap between hardware and software.

I current pursuing my B.S. in Computer Engineering from the University of Pittsburgh, where I am developing a solid foundation in relevant topics such as wireless communication, system microarchitecture, and embedded systems. Over the years, I’ve had the opportunity to work on FPGA-based and microcontroller-based embedded systems, database structuring and management, front-end web development, and my work has been driven by a commitment to building efficient, scalable, and reliable systems.
